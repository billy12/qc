## The Recommendation We Built

**Direction chosen: Fix 1 + Fix 2**

### Fix 1 — Activate the Raffle
The raffle ticket mechanic already exists (given for picking animal race winners) but there is no announced Grand Prize Drawing tied to it. Tickets currently feel like trinkets with no payoff.

**Recommendation:** Announce a Grand Prize Drawing at 12:15 PM from the main stage. Source the prize through local business sponsorships (zero cost to the Town). Families now have a scheduled reason to stay through the final hour.

### Fix 2 — The Post-Hunt Handoff
The 30 seconds after a family finishes their age-group egg hunt is when they decide to stay or leave. Currently: no staff, no signage, no programming targets that moment.

**Recommendation:** When each age-group hunt concludes, hunt staff transition to handoff ambassadors at the hunt exit — directing families toward the next race heat. No new hires; redeployment of existing staff.

**Why these two together:** Fix 1 gives families a reason to stay. Fix 2 captures them at the exact moment before they drift to the parking lot.

---

## Three Key Research Findings (Slide 4 in the deck)

1. **The raffle loop is broken** — tickets exist, no announced drawing
2. **Activities run in silos** — egg hunts, races, Play Zone, EggScape are not connected by any event-wide narrative or flow
3. **The post-hunt moment is unclaimed** — egg hunts are already staggered well; the problem is per-wave drift after each group finishes, not one mass exodus
